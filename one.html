Kevin Durant

  • Base Set (Series 3)

Moment Details

In this moment, a double team attempts to defend against NBA Superstar Kevin Durant in the corner. However, Durant takes his defender off the dribble, sets up on the baseline, and buries an untouchable fadeaway.

NFT Details

Why it's my favourite

This is one of the first moments I got on TopShot. As part of the Limited-Edition Kevin Durant Starter Pack, a limited number of starter packs would get a Kevin Durant moment in them.

Starter Packs are a pack of Moment NFTs that are exclusively available for new collectors who haven't had a chance to open their first pack yet.

Zach Lavine

  • Dunk
  • Game Recognize Game (Series 3)
  • Common #5104/10000

Moment Details

Zach Lavine has some of the most exciting slams in NBA. In this dunk, he sizes up his defender, makes a swift spin move to get around him, and goes up for a powerful, one-handed dunk that rocks the rim.

NFT Details

Why it's my favourite

This moment is one of Klay Thompson's favourite NBA highlights. The Klay Thompson Game Recognize Game Pack included six hand-picked and narrated moments by Klay Thompson of players with the best NBA comeback stories - one of which is this moment.

As part of TopShot's partnership with Klay, a portion of the proceeds from each pack was donated to the Thompson Family Foundation's 941 Project. The 941 Project to assist and support re-entry to the workplace for individuals from disadvantaged situations.
